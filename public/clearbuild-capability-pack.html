The process

How the audit works

The audit runs over 3 to 4 weeks. No workshops, no consultants camping in your office. Structured remote interviews, a clear analysis framework, and a defined set of deliverables at the end.

Stage 01
Discovery
Weeks 1–2

Structured interviews with 4 to 8 members of your team. We map actual workflows, not an org chart. We find where time is being spent and where it is being wasted.

Stage 02
Analysis
Weeks 2–3

Every finding is costed. We use your billing rates and headcount to convert time waste into a number your finance director can read.

Stage 03
Prioritisation
Week 3

Opportunities are ranked: impact versus ease of implementation. You get a clear picture of what to do first, second, and what to leave for later.

Stage 04
Delivery
Weeks 3–4

The findings are presented and a full set of deliverables handed over. Not a conceptual strategy: a practical action plan you can act from immediately.
